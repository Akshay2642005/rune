# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We expect all contributors to:
- Communicate respectfully and constructively
- Focus on technical merit over personal opinions
- Be open to feedback and iteration
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, trolling, or abusive language
- Dismissing others without technical reasoning
- Posting sensitive or private information
- Any conduct that disrupts collaboration

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, reject contributions, or ban participants who violate these rules.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to the maintainers.
